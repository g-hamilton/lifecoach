Promote Your eCourse Help with promoting your eCourse

Promote your eCourse as widely as you can! Hit the share button to share your eCourse landing page on social media, or copy the link and paste it into your website or any of your promotional posts or emails. This link will take clients to your eCourse landing page where they will be able to enroll in this eCourse. You also pay a lower commission (earn a higher % per sale) on all enrollments when you use this link. Learn more about promoting your eCourse and the shared payment model.
