How Lifecoach Works

1. Profile

Start with a free profile

Here at Lifecoach, everything starts with a free coach profile. Add a friendly video intro, showcase your products & services & collect leads.

2. Products & Services

Add your products and services

Create coaching services, coaching programs and even eCourses. We'll guide you every step of the way through creation, publishing & promotion. We'll even promote your products & services actively through our global network of promotional partners to reach new clients you may not normally reach.

3. Discovery

Take bookings & convert leads

Prospective clients book discovery video sessions directly on your calendar or message you from your landing pages. See all your leads in one place, answer questions and ensure clients are right for you before inviting them to enrol. When they do enrol, we make payment a breeze.

4. Coach

Coach online without borders

Coach clients online through the Lifecoach video platform. Schedule sessions, send payment requests, share resources and take notes. Keep track of every interaction between client sessions with our lightweight CRM.

Active Promotion

Leverage Our Global Partner Network

We actively select & promote relevant coaches, services & eCourses to target audiences all over the world through our global network of promotional partners. Our mission is to reach the people who need your services the most, so you can spend less time marketing and more time transforming people's lives for the better. Included as standard when you join our community.
