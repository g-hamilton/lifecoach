Build Your Own Links

Step 1:
Prepare a link to Lifecoach

Prepare to share any Lifecoach link. You can choose to link to any page on Lifecoach, including our home page, individual coach pages, search pages or product & service pages.

Notes:

Be sure that your chosen link includes the lifecoach.io domain. For example: https://lifecoach.io or https://lifecoach.io/programs can both be shared.

Step 2:
Add your code

Add your unique tracking code to the end of your chosen link.

Notes:

For example, if you want to send your audience to the page lifecoach.io/programs and your unique tracking code is {{ partnerF.trackingCode.value }} , you would share the following full link: lifecoach.io/programs{{ partnerF.trackingCode.value }}

Step 3:
Share anywhere

Share your full link anywhere.

Notes:

It doesn't matter where your audience go after visiting your initial link, we will attempt to track them over single or multiple visits & credit you with any successful sale.
